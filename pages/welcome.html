Bulwark is protecting you

Ads, trackers, and distracting sites are being blocked. No account, no telemetry.

1

Pin the bull to your toolbar

Click the puzzle icon in Chrome's toolbar, then pin Bulwark so it is one click away.

2

Toggle protection anytime

Click the icon to pause blocking on a site, see what got blocked, or hide an element. Or press Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + B on any page.

3

Make it yours

Open Settings to block distracting sites on a schedule, add custom filter lists, turn on tracking protection, and pick a theme.
